💪Why Your Body Needs Exercise

Your body is like a supercar the more you use it, the stronger it gets! Kids who move for 60 minutes every day are more happier, sleep better, and do better at school.

✨Quick Tips for Healthy Habits

  • Eat colorful fruits and veggies
  • Drink plenty of water
  • Get enough sleep every night
  • Wash your hands regularly
