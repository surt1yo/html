🌍 Planet Earth: A Quick Guide

Our planet is full of incredible records, species, and facts. Here is a quick guide using lists!

Steps to Protect Our Planet:

  1. Switch off lights when you leave a room
  2. Use a reusable water bottle every day
  3. Walk or cycle instead of driving
  4. Plant a tree in your neighbourhood
  5. Spread awareness -- talk to your friends

Incredible Natural Wonders:

  • Amazon Rainforest -- home to 10% of all species on Earth
  • Great Barrier Reef -- the largest coral reef system in the world
  • Mount Everest -- the tallest mountain on Earth
  • Grand Canyon -- a 277-mile long natural wonder carved by the Colorado River
  • Victoria Falls -- one of the largest and most famous waterfalls in the world

Key Terms:

Climate Change
The long-term change in Earth's climate, primarily caused by human activities such as burning fossil fuels.
Renewable Energy
Energy derived from natural sources that are replenished at a higher rate than they are consumed, such as solar, wind, and hydro power.
Biodiversity
The variety of life in a particular habitat or ecosystem, including the number of different species and their genetic variation.
